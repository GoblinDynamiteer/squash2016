FÖR UART KOMMUNIKATION:

UG - GND / JORD (UART / GPIO Select - Tie to ground & reset the board for UART Mode)
VIN - VOLTAGE IN (3.3V - 5V)
RX - RECIEVE (SERIAL IN -> To Soundboard - 3.3V Logic)
TX - TRANSMIT (SERIAL OUT -> From Soundboard - 3-5V Logic)
RST - RESET
ACT - ACTIVITY PIN (Goes low when audio is playing)
